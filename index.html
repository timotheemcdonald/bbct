Virtual Worlds

What is a Virtual World?

A Virtual World differs from a traditional game in a few ways. Let's take a look at three main differences.

The first is that a Virtual World is persistent over time, and players can affect change to their surroundings - just like the real world! This can be done in different ways depending on the game, such as planting a tree, building a house, or becoming the ruler of a city!

The second thing to know, and this is a big difference, is that Virtual Worlds have no win condition. In a traditional game, there is a goal or task that - once completed - a winner is declared and the game ends.
